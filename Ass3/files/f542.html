CONTRACT RELEASE C13-044
NASA Selects Operations and Maintenance Services for West Virginia Site

NASA has selected West Virginia University Research Corporation (WVURC) of Morgantown, W.Va., to provide operations and maintenance services at the agency’s Independent Verification and Validation (IV&V) Facility in Fairmont, W.Va.

The total value of this cost-no-fee, indefinite-delivery, indefinite -quantity contract is $40 million. The five-year performance period begins Sept. 30 and ends Sept. 29, 2018.

WVURC will operate and maintain NASA's IV&V Facility, providing institutional, groundskeeping and janitorial services; information technology services and equipment; and facilities maintenance.
