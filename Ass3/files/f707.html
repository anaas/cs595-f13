Hot Rod Teddy - A Lesson in Himility (KIT-SP02305)

by RICHARD SMITH

price: Php 100.00
weight: 100 g
size: 9 X 7 inches
no of. pages: 24
ISBN: 978-971-004-177-0

 

  • Sypnosis
  • Editorial Reviews
  • Customer Reviews
  • About the Author
 Hot Rod Teddy is a rising star in the world of toy animal car racing. Slowly though, pride sneaks up on him and swells his plush little head with his new-found fame. Join Teddy and his good friends, Perky Pup and Terry Hound, as they embark on a journey that puts even their strong friendship to the test! This book is intended for little ones 4 to 7 years old as they enjoy themselves on the laps of their reading parents while learning the value of humility.
